How to Compress Images Online

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking "Select Files"
  2. Adjust settings - choose compression level and output format
  3. Click "Compress Image" and wait a few seconds
  4. Download your optimized image with reduced file size

Why Compress Images?

Image compression helps reduce file sizes for faster website loading, lower bandwidth usage, and improved storage efficiency. Our tool maintains visual quality while significantly reducing the file size.
